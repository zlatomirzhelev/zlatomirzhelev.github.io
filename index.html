Solution Manuals

Why write solution manuals?

No one can solve all the problems out there (mainly due to time constraints). Many books don't have solutions or answers to problems. Some people are discourged to work through books with no solution manual, some enjoy it. I believe that we should create a network of solutions for the following reasons:
  • 1. Those, who enjoy working through books, won't lose a lot of time writing them up in .pdf format. In this way struggling people can use them.
  • 2. Writing up solutions, makes one think more deeply about the problem.
  • 3. Sometimes one wants to see a specific technique or look at how somebody else has solved the problem.
